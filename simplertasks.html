Here's a simple guide to use SimplerTasks:

  • Use "!" at the beginning for high priority tasks.
  • Use "-" at the beginning for low priority tasks.
  • Use "x" at the beginning for completed tasks.
  • Use "#context" to categorize tasks, like #home or #work.
  • Use "@name" to assign tasks, like @Rob or @Alice.
  • Tasks can also include links, which will be clickable in the fancy view.
